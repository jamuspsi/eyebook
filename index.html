Our Printer is PrintNinja

One of the things I talk about most with other webcomic creators is, "who prints your books?" I always wholeheartedly recommend PrintNinja.


Since starting in webcomics back in 2001, I've used a number of different printers. Finding a good one is hard to do.

I always figured that finding a great one was impossible, but PrintNinja is just outstanding on price, print quality, and customer service.

So if you're looking to print a book, especially if it's full color, then I'd start by getting a bid from PrintNinja. They'll be the one to beat for price, and I seriously don't think anyone can beat them for service.
